Up and running in three steps

No global installs, no version conflicts. Just drop the wrapper into your repo.

Grab the wrapper

Copy dtcw into your project root and commit it alongside your sources.

Write your docs

Add AsciiDoc files under src/docs — arc42 templates included out of the box.

Generate & publish

Run ./dtcw local generateSite and ship a polished microsite to any host.
